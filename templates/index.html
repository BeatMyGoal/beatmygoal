Welcome to BeatMyGoal!

BeatMyGoal is a new platform to manage and track your goals, and compete with your friends to reach them.

Steps:
  • Create an account.
  • Set your first goal, for example, "Learn to cook homemade Italian food". Set a time-limit or a goal number you want to reach.
  • Set a prize for when (not, if) you reach your goal!
  • Invite your friends to join in and compete. The highest achiever can win the prize.
  • Login frequently to log and share your progress!
